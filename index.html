About LakeBasin AgriTech

LakeBasin AgriTech is an emerging production, aggregation, and value-addition enterprise in Kasama and Mbala. We integrate technology-driven farming with structured markets to strengthen food systems. Our model connects smallholder farmers to formal markets through quality processing, transparent pricing, and reliable supply chains across Northern Zambia.

Vision

Northern Zambia's leading integrated agri-business. Driving modern agriculture through technology, sustainable practices, and market integration to transform rural livelihoods and strengthen regional food security.

Mission

Deliver high-quality products through technology-enabled production. We partner with communities to create sustainable value chains, improve farmer incomes, and supply consistent, high-quality agricultural products to local and export markets.

Our 4 Pillars

Our integrated model reduces supply gaps, improves farmer incomes, and supplies formal markets with consistent, high-quality produce.

Commercial Production

Solar irrigation & mechanisation. Digital monitoring systems ensure year-round supply with consistent quality. Our center pivot systems and precision agriculture techniques maximize yield while conserving resources.

Aggregation & Partnerships

Structured markets & out-grower schemes. We work directly with hundreds of smallholder farmers, providing fair pricing, quality standards, and extension support to build sustainable partnerships.

Value Addition

Grading, cleaning & processing. Our packhouse operations include washing, grading, drying, milling, and dehydrated vegetable processing to extend shelf-life and increase market value.

Trading & Distribution

Supplying supermarkets & export markets. We connect quality produce to retail chains, wholesale buyers, institutions, and regional export markets through reliable logistics and cold chain management.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Investing in farmer training, youth employment & community resilience. Our CSR programs focus on climate-smart agriculture practices, skills development, and creating sustainable livelihoods that benefit entire communities across Northern Zambia.

Farmer Training & Extension

Hands-on training & digital extension. We provide practical workshops, field demonstrations, and mobile-based advisory services to help farmers improve yields, reduce losses, and increase their incomes through better agricultural practices.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Market-linked opportunities for youth & women. We create employment and entrepreneurship opportunities in agribusiness, providing training, mentorship, and access to markets that enable youth and women to build sustainable livelihoods.
